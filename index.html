About Me

Two-year starter at quarterback for coach Craig Stump at Beaumont West Brook High School. ... Named the District 21-5A Most Valuable Offense Player and to The Port Arthur News Super Team as a senior in 2008. ... Recognized by Dave Campbell's Texas Football Magazine as the Most Valuable Offensive Player in District 21-5A for the 2008 season. ... Was a member of The Beaumont Enterprise Super Gold Team as a junior in 2007. ... Threw for 1,579 yards on 105-of-173 accuracy for a completion percentage of .607 in 2008. ... Threw 12 touchdown passes and also rushed for 102 yards and an additional two TDs. ... Passed for 2,018 yards and 16 TDs as a junior in 2007. ... Helped the Bruins to the area round of the state playoffs with a 9-2 overall record and 4-1 mark in District 21-5A as a senior. ... During his junior season, Bruins went 10-2 overall and 7-0 in District 21-5A and also reached the area round of the playoffs. ... Also recruited by Sam Houston State, where his father Arthur was a star quarterback after a standout career at Beaumont's South Park High School.
